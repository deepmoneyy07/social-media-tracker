## 3. Messaging Frameworks

### Recognition Philosophy

**Problem:** Top-down recognition feels corporate. It doesn't stick.

**Insight:** Peer recognition feels genuine because it's voluntary, specific, and frequent.

**Evidence:**
- Peer recognition adoption: 60%+ (vs. 20% top-down)
- Retention improvement: 18-23%
- Engagement lift: 22-25 points
- Company feel: "How we work here" (vs. "HR initiative")

**Solution:** Peer-first, values-tied, frictionless recognition culture.

### Culture Transformation Arc

1. **Before:** Top-down program → Low adoption (20%) → Feels forced → Culture unchanged
2. **After:** Peer-to-peer recognition → High adoption (60%+) → Feels authentic → Culture shifts
3. **Metrics That Matter:** Not programs launched, but people who feel valued daily

### Adoption Framework (3 Parts)

1. **Peer-First Launch**
   - Start with 10-15 early adopters recognizing each other
   - Let it spread peer-to-peer (viral adoption)
   - Don't mandate from top (feels forced)

2. **Values-Tied Recognition**
   - "Who lived our values this week?" (not "Recognize your best performer")
   - Reinforces culture, not just performance
   - Makes recognition tied to identity, not metrics

3. **Frictionless Design**
   - Mobile-first, one-click nominations
   - Must take <30 seconds (or adoption dies)
   - Async options (not real-time only)
   - Instant gratification + feedback loop

### Differentiators vs. Competitors

**vs. Snap Surveys / TinPulse / Officevibe:**
- VC: Culture-first + peer recognition + rewards all-in-one
- Not just engagement measurement, but culture change
- Employee voice + recognition + rewards ecosystem

**vs. Bonusly / Kudos:**
- VC: Global (US, India, Europe, APAC)
- VC: Recognition + surveys + wellness + gifting (integrated)
- VC: Pricing more accessible to mid-market

**vs. Simple platforms:**
- VC: Enterprise-grade (governance, compliance, security)
- VC: Multi-language support (EN, FR, ES, DE)
- VC: Deep integration (ADP, SAP, etc.)

### Content Guidelines

**Headings:**
- H1: Only one per post (the title)
- H2: Main sections (3-5 per post)
- H3: Sub-sections (under each H2)
- H4: Detail level (minimal use)

**Length:**
- Blog posts: 1500-3000+ words
- Lead magnet guides: 2000-3000 words
- Quick takes: 500-800 words

**Links:**
- Internal: 3-5 links to other blog posts
- External: 2-3 credible sources (research backing)
- Product mentions: 1-2 (natural, not forced)

**Keywords:**
- Primary: In title, H2, intro, and 2-3x body
- Secondary: In H3s and supporting sections
- Avoid: Keyword stuffing, repetition

**Formatting:**
- Bold: Key concepts, important takeaways
- Lists: Numbered (steps) or bullets (options)
- Blockquotes: Original research, expert insights
- Tables: Data comparison, feature breakdowns

### Image Standards

**Featured Image:**
- Size: 1200x630px (Facebook/social optimal)
- Format: PNG or JPEG (optimize for web)
- Source: Cloudinary CDN
- URL Pattern: `https://cdn.vantagecircle.com/image/upload/q_auto,f_auto/path/to/image.png`

**Inline Images:**
- Alt text: Descriptive (accessibility + SEO)
- Captions: Optional but helpful
- Optimization: Cloudinary auto-optimization

## 8. Workflow: Blog → Social Repurposing

### Per Blog Post, Create:

**LinkedIn Authority Post (AIDA)**
- Time: 10-15 min
- Hook: Problem statement or surprising stat
- Interest: Why it matters for HR leaders
- Desire: Outcome they'll get
- Action: Low-friction CTA (reply, comment)
- Link: Blog post URL

**Reddit Deep-Dive (PAS)**
- Time: 20-30 min
- Problem: Specific challenge (adoption, retention, culture)
- Agitation: Why it's worse than people think
- Solution: Framework shared freely
- Subreddit: r/humanresources (primary)
- Engagement: 100% replies in first 2 hours

**Twitter Thread (Hot Take)**
- Time: 15-20 min
- Hook: Contrarian statement or insight
- Expansion: 6-7 tweets building the argument
- Proof: Data point or example
- CTA: "What's your take?"

**Instagram Reel Script**
- Time: 25-30 min
- Hook: First 2 seconds stop the scroll
- Problem: Relatable challenge
- Insight: "Aha" moment or reframe
- Solution: Better way forward
- CTA: Comment or DM

**Email Campaign**
- Time: 20-30 min
- Subject: Compelling + keyword
- Preview: First 40 characters intrigue
- Body: Blog excerpt + link + CTA
- Lead magnet: Downloadable PDF version
